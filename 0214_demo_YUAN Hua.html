Introduction

The course introduces students to the fundamental knowledge of AI to develop interactive media, especially games, with intelligent non-player characters for an engaging gameplay. Different types of AI algorithms in the context of game design such as movement, decision-making, pathfinding, and tactical and strategy AI will be covered.

Learning Outcomes

  • Explain the basic concepts and principles of AI algorithms used in game design
  • Evaluate and apply the appropriate AI algorithms in different game design contexts
  • Implement Implement AI algorithms effectively to deliver real-time performance for an engaging gameplay
  • Develop new solutions through integration of different AI algorithms in a creative game design project
